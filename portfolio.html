The question we had was: What is the demographic of the House of Representatives over the years? We made a graph that illustrated that, and it shows that from 2008-2010, it was a majority of democrats, and from 2012-2018, the house was a majority of republicans until 2020, when the house change and became democratic majority again.

We got our results from https://history.house.gov/Institution/Party-Divisions/Party-Divisions/. We decided to use a bar graph, because it can represent multiple years and it would allow us to compare the number of democrats vs. republicans in the house easily. This graph was based on a normal bar graph, and we just used code used in a normal bar graph for our bar graph.
