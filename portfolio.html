Portfolio

We each worked on our games together, but we ended up making three different games because we wanted to do three different things. We all worked on the Pong game, but Yui and I wanted to make a story-based game, so we each made a story based game too.

In the Duck game, you try to collect food.

In the Pong game, you try to hit the ball with the green paddle. Try not to let it hit the floor!

In the Bear game, the bear is lost and you need to get home! There are multiple ways you can get home.



We made a dice roll app. It can roll up to six dice, and it has a guessing feature, where you can try to guess the dice roll's value. You can roll the dice by pressing the roll button or shaking your electronic. Click on the dice to go to mitapinventor.com and see the code!


We made a Rags to Riches game, called The Game of Life. You're born into poverty, and through a series of decisions, you can become a billionaire!


We made a Lyrics Guessing Game, where you have to guess the song title based on the lyrics you are given. You can pick from four options.


We manipulated the picture of two pikas, first inverting the colors of the entire image, then using a color range, we "selected" the snow and made it yellow (don't eat the yellow snow).
